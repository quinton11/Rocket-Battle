space rocket game.
Main objective was to set up a game to train algorithm on and compete with it.
Competing in the sense of either highscore leaderboard or versus.
For highscore leaderboard, its how long you can survive and defeat enemies,
gain power ups and increase points.
For the versus, AI plays against you and other cpu controlled opponents.
Last man standing is the winner.
It could be made into a 5 round or 3 round set where majority wins makes
one the winner.
